CHALLENGED LITERATURE

To Kill a Mockingbird by Harper Lee

"You never really understand a person until you consider things from his point of view... until you climb into his skin and walk around in it."

Banned for racial slurs, depictions of racism, and perceived white savior themes. Often challenged for making people feel "uncomfortable."

I Know Why the Caged Bird Sings by Maya Angelou

"There is no greater agony than bearing an untold story inside you."

Challenged for depictions of sexual abuse, racism, and offensive language. Considered too explicit in some school districts.
