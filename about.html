ABOUT
At Unbound Pages, we believe that banning books is an assault on the freedom to read, learn, and grow. Our mission is to highlight the stories behind the bans, the people affected, and the path to a future where every book can be shared without fear.

From grassroots advocacy to nationwide awareness campaigns, our community works tirelessly to expose censorship and champion the free exchange of ideas. Join us in ensuring that no page remains forbidden.
